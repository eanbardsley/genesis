Abstract
Through Newton’s Universal Law of Gravity and the ﬁne structure constant we can introduce
the concepts of proton-seconds which are the number of protons over a period of time.
The ﬁne structure constant allows for this because it produces close to integer values with
Newton’s equation for gravity I ﬁnd, where Carbon and Boron are concerned, carbon at the
center of life as we know it. The ﬁne structure constant is somewhat of a mystery in that it
is not derived, but was discovered cropping up all over nature by Sommerfeld. This results
in a mass on the order of the astroidal meteoroids that can make it to the Earth surface
before burning up in the lower limit. The predicted mass to the mass of such a meteoroid
is Venus orbit in astronomical units. Venus is thought to be a failed Earth.
